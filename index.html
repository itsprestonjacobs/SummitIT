Who we are

Local tech experts who treat you like a neighbor

Summit IT started with a simple belief: good tech help should be friendly, clear, and actually there when you need it — no call centers, no jargon, no runaround.

We help people and small businesses get past the tech problems that slow them down — from a laptop that won't start to a Wi-Fi network that keeps dropping. Some issues take two minutes in chat; others need a real set of eyes. Either way, we meet you where you are.

On a video call you can point your camera at the device and we'll work through the fix together, step by step. No mailing anything off, no waiting days — just real solutions from people who genuinely like solving this stuff.
